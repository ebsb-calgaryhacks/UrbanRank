We decided to create a project utilizing Calgary's open data tool. In response to the steep increase in housing costs in Calgary, we developed a solution to simplify the process of finding suitable living spaces for local residents.

About Us:

The project:

Our project enables users to discover the ideal neighborhood in Calgary that aligns with their preferences. Users complete a form, and our algorithm finds the best matching communities.
